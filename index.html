HAN HUANG

I currently work as a graphic designer for South China Morning Post, a Hong Kong-based publication. I use maps, data visualisations and photos to cover a range of topics in China and Asia.

Previously,  I was a Graphic Journalist at Reuters News Agency, where I developed interactive visuals as well as static graphics on current events and financial markets.

Prior to that, I graduated from the University of Miami with a master’s degree in journalism. This website showcases some of my recent works.
