Chinese Americans

#Responsive #D3.js #jQuery #topojson #AfterEffect #Illustrator #Adobe Audition

This is my thesis project at the University of Miami! I made an animation to explain the immigration story of early Chinese Americans through Illustrator and After Effects. Then I added a timeline to illustrate important events after American Civil War. To show their current life in the United States, I created various charts and graphics to demonstrate the population distribution in each state, the percentage of different groups of visas held by Chinese Americans, as well as the remittance flows between China and other regions.

Autism

#Responsive #D3.js #jQuery #Bootstrap #JSON

This project idea came with a documentary I did on kids with special needs. There is a timeline to show important steps our society achieved on Autism syndrome. I used D3 library to generate interactive graphics while illustrating the prevalence, types, and the ratios of race and gender of this syndrome. In addition, I also focused on the financial burden of families with autism children as I found family members had to invest large amounts of money and provided meticulous care for autistic kid when I spend time with my documentary subjects.

Colonoscopy

#Illustrator

This is a project I worked for my infographic class. My task was to redesign graphics and found more data based on a report published in New York Times. To find a better way to display these data and enhance the readability, I only retained important paragraphs and emphasized on the expenditure of every process during a colposcopy and the percentage of patient numbers from different types of groups.

Climate Change

#Illustrator

This infographic is taking about beef consumption and Climate Change. I chose New York Times as my client and decided to explain how meat production and climate change are related. To narrow down the topic, I focused on the amounts of different kinds of meat consumed in the United States compared to other major countries and the sources of emissions from beef production. I also analyzed the pollution produced during the process of raising, processing, transporting and cooking livestock products.

Emoji

#Illustrator #Quadrigram

This website built in quadrigram with hundreds of static graphics created in illustrator. I used the data on the SwiftKey Emoji Report to demonstrate the way people from different backgrounds play emoji in their daily life and some exciting findings about emoji that most people did not know. Some data may seem unbelievable but it is really a lovely project!

White Shark Predation

#Hype #Maya #Illustrator

This is a group project under the supervision of professor Alberto Cairo from School of Communication and Professor Neil Hammerschlag from Rosenstiel Marine School. My team members and I developed scientific explanations on the white shark predation behavior. My role in this project was to build a 3D model of white Shark. We finished the 3D animation in Maya and built the website with a software named Tumult Hype.

A 91-year-old Ballerina

#Canon EOS 5D #Premiere

This story is for the celebration of the 100th anniversary of the novel named Alice's Adventures in Wonderland published in the world. Our task was to find a subject named Alice and she had to have an interesting story. I traveled to a ballet studio in Boca Raton and found a lovely lady, Alice Modine. Alice is the oldest student in this studio but still keeps having three ballet classes and two yoga classes each week even she is 91 years old.

Kids with Special needs

#Canon EOS 5D #Premiere

This is a long-term documentary focused on kids with special needs. Jessica is a high school student who devotes most of her time on best buddies, a non-profit organization that aims to help people with intellectual and developmental disabilities. To feature her friendship with her two best buddies, I traveled to Broward each weekend by train rail and lived in her house to record every wonderful moment.

Shake-A-Leg

#Canon EOS 5D #Premiere

Shake-A-Leg (SALM) is a non-profit organization working with people with physical and economic challenges in a marine environment. My subject is Woody Beckham, a university student who became paralyzed in a sport competition. Because of his optimism and determination, he is now a board member of Woody foundation, a not for profit aims to raise funds for the treatment of people with same injuries.
